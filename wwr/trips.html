Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for the rafting trips?

Wear comfortable clothing that can get wet. We recommend quick-drying materials and water shoes.

Is there an age limit for the trips?

Yes, Adventure Rapids is suitable for ages 12 and up, while Family Fun Float is open to all ages.

Do I need to know how to swim?

While it's not required to know how to swim, it is highly recommended for your safety. All participants will be provided with life jackets.
